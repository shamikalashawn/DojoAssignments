About Me!

I was born and raised the oldest of four children in San Antonio, TX. Besides remembering the Alamo, I spent a lot of time as a kid reading and going to engineering and math camps. I made my first website in the 10th grade and learned basic html in one sitting. That (albiet dated) website can be perused here. I studied African and African American Studies at Stanford University and was the first in my family to graduate high school and college. I went on to grad school at a seminary to explore what social justice looked like in the digital space. My senior thesis is housed here. Now that I have looked at technology and ethics as well as the black experience academically, I want to make a positive impact on the tech industry! If you know of ways to help me do that, please contact me via email here!
